<p>We may collect the following information to provide and improve app functionality:</p>

<ul>

</ul>


<h2>Image and AI Processing</h2>

<p>

Images uploaded to Hair AI Salon are processed exclusively to generate the requested visual result

(such as hairstyle or hair color changes).
