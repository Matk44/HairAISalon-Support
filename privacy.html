Privacy Policy

Hair AI Salon

Last updated: January 4, 2026

Hair AI Salon ("the App", "we", "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information to provide and improve app functionality:

  • Email address – Used for authentication and account management
  • User-generated content – Images uploaded for hairstyle or hair color processing
  • Subscription and purchase information – Managed by the Apple App Store and Google Play Store
  • Anonymous usage and diagnostic data – Including crash reports and performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Process hairstyle and hair color transformations
  • Enable access to premium features and manage credit usage
  • Improve app stability, performance, and user experience

Third-Party AI and Data Sharing

Important Disclosure: Images uploaded to Hair AI Salon are processed using third-party AI services to generate hairstyle and hair color transformations. By using these features, you explicitly consent to your images being shared with our AI processing partners for this specific purpose.

We want to be transparent about how your images are handled:

  • Images are sent to third-party AI providers solely to generate your requested hairstyle or hair color results
  • Images are not used for advertising purposes
  • Images are not used to train AI models
  • Images are not shared with any other third parties beyond our AI processing partners
  • Images are not retained after processing, except where temporarily required to deliver the requested feature

Before using any AI-powered feature, you will be asked to provide explicit permission for this data sharing.

Data Linked to You

The following data may be linked to your identity:

  • Email address
  • Subscription status
  • In-app generated results associated with your account

Anonymous analytics and diagnostic data collected for performance improvement is not linked to your personal identity.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase, including Firebase Authentication and Firestore. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Firebase may collect limited device information, crash logs, and analytics data as part of its service delivery and security operations. Please refer to Google Firebase's privacy documentation for more information.

Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your data is only shared with third parties as explicitly described in this policy:

  • AI Processing Partners: Images are shared with third-party AI services to generate your requested transformations (with your explicit consent)
  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and analytics services
  • Payment Processors: Apple App Store and Google Play Store for managing subscriptions and purchases

Tracking

Hair AI Salon does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies and does not use data for targeted advertising or tracking purposes.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide app functionality or meet legal obligations. When you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from our systems, typically within 30 days.

Data Deletion

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time directly within the App by selecting "Delete My Account" in the Settings or Profile section.

This action is irreversible and will permanently remove all your personal data from our systems.

Children's Privacy

Hair AI Salon is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If we become aware that personal data from a child under 13 has been collected, we will delete it promptly. We ask users to confirm their age before creating an account, in accordance with Apple App Store guidelines.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable data protection laws.
